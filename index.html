Quick glance at Classes

Class - it's not just how you beat up your enemy, it's also everything else - your vibe, story, your views and goals.

Barbarian
You like to just destroy things, roaring for no reason, being too strong and being honest in your emotions? Barbarians have no reason to not rage. It's basically how they get most power and becoming invincible.
Fighter
Basic. Take a sword and give them a good fight. Fighters are actually most complex in their history, from soldier, or knight of honour, to the slave, that fights now for their own good or people.
Monk
This guys just be like "we are going not to slay you, but beat up to your death just cause we can". And they can, cause they learn martial arts for years. They don't need rage to be overpowered. They're calm as hell.
Ranger
It's kinda like hunters, but they can hunt your arse and put you down. Something between Rogue and Druid, but they mostly have their own moral rules, that not sounds like "NATURE IS IMPORTANT!!!" or "Gimme money or else die". Mostly "Nah, I wanna some get some beer and drink it with my bear".
Druid
Yeah, they don't need personal bear, they can be a bear. Nature lovers, hippies or primitive anarchists. Mostly have random skills, like some monk things, some magic, and mostly have a really basic or shady backgrounds. They were or children of Druid or fascinating path on becoming them.
RogueRogue
Its what you think - skunks,whores beggars, liars, shady merchants. Of course, they don't have parents and had a really shitty background and path full of despair. Now they can fool you and backstab you because they have +70 for stealth
Bard
The other rogue - a prick in the arse, that can say hilarious or mean stuff and kill you instantly. There is magic in their mouth and hands, but instead and everything because they're harismatic. And also because they cannot stop their libido.
Cleric
A priest with a bonk-machine and a good healer. Mostly are really religious and follows rules of their god properly. But not all gods are just like Jesus, there is Tricksters, hehe. So, they can heal you and then steal your money just because they're followers of some greedy golden god. But still nice healers.
Paladin
A knights of honour and order - they've rules and they need to follow them, cause it's makes them stronger. Mostly too lawful good, that scares.
Wizard
Magic nerds that chose to learn from others how to do basic things like light a candle with bare hands. Mostly really smart and wise, but really weak people. Golden standard achievement: "got unconscious in first fight". Never gets old.
Warlock
Daddy issues. The old god or whatever gives them power and they're chill. Totally some cult followers, or forever in bargain with some demon, that saved their life. Never see anything wrong to have magic from others, especially when they can use it after short rest fully again.
Sorcerer
Most chaotic, you better be careful. Their magic is something from their blood, their lineage and they're just like a match - someday will burn to the ashes too quick.
